üìã Interview One-Liners

Quick Answers

"What is VM?"
"Continuous process to find, prioritize, and fix security weaknesses before attackers exploit them."

"VA vs Pentest?"
"VA is the health checkup; pentesting is the surgery. VA finds what's vulnerable; pentest proves what's exploitable."

"Why not just use CVSS?"
"CVSS measures severity, not risk. A CVSS 9.8 on an isolated lab box matters less than a CVSS 7.0 on your payment gateway."

"What's EPSS?"
"Predicts likelihood a vuln will be exploited in the next 30 days. Helps prioritize real threats over theoretical ones."

"What's CISA KEV?"
"List of vulnerabilities actively exploited in the wild. If it's on KEV, fix it NOW - attackers are already using it."
