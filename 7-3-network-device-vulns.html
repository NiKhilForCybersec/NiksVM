âš ï¸ Edge Device Priority

VPN concentrators, SSL-VPN appliances, and internet-facing firewalls are extremely high priority targets. They're directly exposed to the internet and provide access to internal networks. Treat any vulnerability in these devices as P1 regardless of CVSS.

ğŸ’¡ Interview Question

"How do you handle vulnerabilities on network infrastructure?"

Sample answer: "Network devices require special handling. I use authenticated scanning with SSH or SNMPv3 for accurate version detection. Edge devices like VPN concentrators get P1 priority regardless of CVSS because of their exposure. Firmware updates are scheduled during maintenance windows with configuration backups ready. For critical vulnerabilities, I implement immediate mitigations like disabling vulnerable features or restricting management access via ACLs while we test and schedule the patch."

Summary

Network device vulnerabilities can compromise entire network segments. Prioritize edge devices, maintain authenticated scanning, and have a firmware update process that balances speed with stability. Subscribe to vendor advisories for your specific equipment.
