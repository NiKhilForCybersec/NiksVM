๐ก Interview Question

"How do you handle compliance reporting for vulnerability management?"

Sample answer: "I maintain audit-ready evidence continuously rather than scrambling before assessments. For PCI, I ensure quarterly ASV scans pass and document all internal scan results with remediation evidence. Every critical/high vulnerability has a ticket showing detection date, remediation date, and re-scan verification. Exceptions are formally documented with compensating controls and CISO approval. I keep a compliance dashboard showing current status against each framework's requirements so we're always assessment-ready."

Summary

Compliance reporting requires continuous evidence collection, not just point-in-time assessments. Understand each framework's specific requirements, maintain audit-ready documentation, and track exceptions formally with compensating controls.
