ğŸ’¡ Interview Question

"What metrics do you put on a VM executive dashboard?"

Sample answer: "Executive dashboards need high-level risk indicators, not technical details. I include: overall vulnerability count with severity breakdown, trend over time showing if we're improving, SLA compliance percentage, CISA KEV count for regulatory attention, and comparison to last month/quarter. I keep it to 5-7 metrics maximum. Executives want to know: 'Are we getting better or worse?' and 'What needs my attention?' Everything else is operational detail."

Summary

Effective dashboards match the audienceâ€”executives need trends and risk posture, operations needs real-time data, teams need their specific SLAs. Less is more; focus on actionable metrics rather than data dumps.
