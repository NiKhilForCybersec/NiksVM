üîç Scanning Analogies

Types of Scanning

Unauthenticated ScanLooking at a house from the street - you see the outside only
Authenticated ScanWalking inside with a key - you see everything inside
Agent-basedHaving a security guard live in each house
AgentlessSecurity patrol that visits periodically

Authenticated scans find 5-10x more vulnerabilities because they can see inside!

üåê Attack Vector as Physical Access

NALP = How Close Must the Attacker Get?

N - NetworkAttacker can be anywhere in the world (most dangerous)
A - AdjacentAttacker must be on your street (same network)
L - LocalAttacker must already be inside your house
P - PhysicalAttacker must physically touch the computer (least dangerous)

üîê CVE vs CWE vs CPE

The Medical Record Analogy

CVE"Patient John has the flu" - specific incident/instance
CWE"Respiratory infection" - category of illness
CPE"John Smith, Age 35, Room 401" - identifying information

CVE-2021-44228 (Log4Shell) is a CVE (specific instance) of CWE-502 (Deserialization) affecting CPE:a:apache:log4j (the product).
